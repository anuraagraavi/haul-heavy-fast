
# Light & Medium Towing Landing Page - Google Ads Campaign

## Overview
A mobile-first, CRO-optimized landing page designed for maximum lead conversion from Google Ads traffic. This page will have 13 strategically ordered sections that address customer psychology, overcome objections, and drive action.

## Customer Psychology Analysis

### Key Resistance Points to Address:
1. **Price Fear** - "Will I get ripped off with hidden fees?"
2. **Trust Deficit** - "Is this company legitimate and reliable?"
3. **Quality Concern** - "Will my vehicle be handled with care?"
4. **Time Anxiety** - "How fast can they get here? I'm stranded!"
5. **Availability Worry** - "Are they even open right now?"
6. **Location Doubt** - "Do they serve my area?"
7. **Communication Gap** - "Will they keep me updated?"
8. **Insurance Question** - "What if something goes wrong?"

## Mobile-First Heatmap Strategy

### Thumb Zone Optimization:
- Primary CTAs positioned in lower 40% of screen (natural thumb reach)
- Sticky bottom CTA bar visible after scrolling 200px
- Large tap targets (minimum 48px) for phone buttons

### F-Pattern Scroll Behavior:
- Critical information in first 3 sections (above-fold priority)
- Headlines aligned left for quick scanning
- Key stats/numbers prominently displayed
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max on mobile)

### Attention Retention Tactics:
- Visual variety every section (images, icons, stats, testimonials)
- Progress indicators showing scroll depth
- Strategic CTAs every 2-3 sections
- Micro-animations on scroll for engagement
