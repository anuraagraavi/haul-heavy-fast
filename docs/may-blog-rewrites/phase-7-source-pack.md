## Official sources — I-880 Hayward

- [Interstate 880 (Wikipedia/Caltrans reference)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interstate_880_(California))
  - 47.22 mi Nimitz Freeway, Santa Clara + Alameda counties
  - Exit 24: Whipple Rd, Industrial Pkwy SW, Dyer St (mile ~23.9)
  - Exit 25: Industrial Parkway (mile ~24.76)
  - Exit 26: SR-92 / San Mateo Bridge (mile ~26.92)
- [CHP Golden Gate Division Oakland Area](https://www.chp.ca.gov/find-an-office/golden-gate-division-oakland-area)
- [Alameda CTC I-880 Whipple/Industrial interchange project](https://www.alamedactc.org/programs-projects/highway-improvement/880whippleindustrial)
- [511 Freeway Assist](https://511.org/driving/assist) — Mon–Fri 5:30 a.m.–7:30 p.m. on many corridors; FSP peak 6–10 a.m. and 3–7 p.m.
- [FSP Bay Area FAQ](https://www.fsp-bayarea.org/faq) — no commercial tows; CHP drop lots only

## Official sources — Fremont bridge corridor

- [511 Dumbarton Bridge](https://511.org/driving/bridges/dumbarton-bridge)
  - SR-84 between Alameda and San Mateo counties
  - Westbound toll only (Fremont-side plaza)
  - All-electronic tolling
- [MTC Dumbarton Bridge](https://mtc.ca.gov/operations/programs-projects/bridges/dumbarton-bridge)
  - 1.6 miles, 3 lanes each direction, bike/pedestrian path
  - Rebuilt 1984; connects Fremont/Newark to Menlo Park
- [Caltrans District 4](https://dot.ca.gov/caltrans-near-me/district-4)
- Distinction: San Mateo Bridge = SR-92 (south of Hayward), not Dumbarton (CA-84)
