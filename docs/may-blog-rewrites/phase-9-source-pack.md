## Official sources — Palo Alto garages

- [Palo Alto Ch. 18.54 Parking Facility Design Standards](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/paloalto/latest/paloalto_ca/0-0-0-81120)
  - Underground parking encouraged for mixed-use projects
  - Stall clearance targets ~7.5 ft in many designs
  - Portal signs often 6'8"–7'0" with lower pipes on spirals
- [City of Palo Alto](https://www.cityofpaloalto.org/)
- [Stanford Transportation](https://transportation.stanford.edu/)

## Official sources — Shoreline Amphitheatre

- [Shoreline Amphitheatre — Know Before You Go](https://www.shorelineamphitheatre.com/know-before-you-go)
  - One Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043
  - Parking opens ~1 hour before gate time
  - Rideshare/taxi pickup: Amphitheatre Parkway across pedestrian path
- [Shoreline Amphitheatre FAQ](https://www.shorelineamphitheatre.com/faq)
  - Accessible parking Lots C & D; overflow Lot A with shuttle
  - Caltrain ~2.5 miles (Mountain View station)
  - Buses/limos: oversized parking Lot C; MVPD on site
