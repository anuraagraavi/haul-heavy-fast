## Official sources — delivery van / GVWR

- [FHWA / AFDC vehicle weight classes](https://afdc.energy.gov/vehicles/weight-classes)
  - Class 1–2: light duty ≤10,000 lbs GVWR
  - Class 3–6: medium duty 10,001–26,000 lbs GVWR
- [FMCSA commercial motor vehicle definition](https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/regulations/cmv/definition-commercial-motor-vehicle)
  - CMV threshold: GVWR or GCWR 10,001+ lbs
