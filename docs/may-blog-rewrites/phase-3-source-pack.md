## Official sources — SMC Sheriff vehicle release

- [Vehicle Releases & Requirements](https://www.smcsheriff.com/vehicle-releases-requirements)
  - Registered owner required
  - Valid DL (or gov photo ID + licensed driver)
  - Plate or case number
  - Fee by impound location (fee schedule on page)
  - Exact cash or credit card
  - After-hours/weekends/holidays: **sheriffs_recordsrequest@smcgov.org**
- [Criminal Records Bureau](https://www.smcsheriff.com/support-services/criminal-records-bureau)
  - Phone: (650) 363-4525
  - Fax: (650) 365-9884
  - Counters: Redwood City HQ, San Carlos, Half Moon Bay, Millbrae

## Official sources — CA-92 / San Mateo Bridge

- [511 Freeway Assist](https://511.org/511phone/assist): bridges/tunnels via Caltrans; FSP peak hours Mon–Fri 6–10 AM and 3–7 PM
- [CHP FAQ](https://www.chp.ca.gov/faq/): 911 if in traffic lanes; otherwise 511 "Freeway Assist"
- [CHP Freeway Service Patrol](https://www.chp.ca.gov/programs-services/services-information/freeway-service-patrol/): Golden Gate Division covers San Mateo County
- Corridor: SR-92 / J. Arthur Younger Bridge (San Mateo-Hayward Bridge); 101 interchange; Foster City high-rise section
- Bridge call boxes: green = voice, red = TTY; wind/noise can block audio—Caltrans may still dispatch on active signal
