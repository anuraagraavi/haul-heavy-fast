## Official sources — SFO airport release

- [SFO parking](https://www.flysfo.com/parking)
- [SFO cell phone waiting lot](https://www.flysfo.com/passengers/ground-transportation/cell-phone-waiting-lot)
  - North McDonnell Rd & San Bruno Ave
  - 60-minute maximum, attended vehicle required, no commercial vehicles
  - Closed 1:00 a.m. to 5:00 a.m.
- **SFPD Airport Bureau:** (650) 821-7111; courtesy phone 1.7111 (garage terms)
- **SFO general:** (800) 435-9736 or (650) 821-8211
- [SFPD Airport Bureau](https://www.sanfranciscopolice.org/your-sfpd/explore-department/airport)
- SFO Rules & Regulations Rule 4.3: disabled/abandoned/improperly parked vehicles removed to official impound at owner expense; release on ID + towing charge + parking fees
- Garage operator (SP Plus / Global Parking): tow info via Airport Police; unmarked stall tows at owner expense
- **Not** city SFMTA impound (415) 865-8200 unless Airport Police confirms transfer
- Parking citations: Office of Parking Violations via pticket.com/SFAIR

## Official sources — 511 Freeway Assist / FSP

- [511 Freeway Assist](https://511.org/driving/assist)
  - Dial 511, say Freeway Assist
  - FSP hours listed Mon–Fri 5:30 a.m.–7:30 p.m. on many corridors
  - Non-FSP hours: CHP Rotational Tow Program (standard rates)
  - Bridges/tunnels routed to Caltrans
- [FSP Bay Area FAQ](https://www.fsp-bayarea.org/faq)
  - Free jump, tire, fuel, radiator, tow to CHP safe location
  - Peak windows typically 6–10 a.m. and 3–7 p.m.
  - No commercial tows; no tips
- [CHP Freeway Service Patrol](https://www.chp.ca.gov/programs-services/services-information/freeway-service-patrol/)
  - Golden Gate Division covers Bay Area counties
