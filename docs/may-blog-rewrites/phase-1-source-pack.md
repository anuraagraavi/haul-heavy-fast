## Official sources

### SJPD Recover Towed Vehicle
- URL: https://www.sjpd.org/i-want-to/contact-us/recover-towed-vehicle
- Auto Desk assistance: **(408) 277-4263** (24/7 except daily 12–2 PM break)
- Recorded info line: **(408) 277-4156**
- Auto Desk address: 201 W. Mission St, San José, CA 95110
- Public counter: Mon–Fri 8 AM–4 PM (effective July 1, 2025)
- Lobby: Mon–Fri 8 AM–5 PM
- Online vehicle release available outside counter hours
- Most cases require SJPD release before tow company releases vehicle
- Release fee payable to City of San Jose (cash, cashier's check, money order, Visa/Mastercard/Discover)
- Unlicensed driver tow: possible 30-day hold per CVC
- Airport parking T&C cites reclaim via **(408) 277-4156**

### SJPD Tow Hearing
- Tow Hearing Unit: **(408) 277-5429**, 201 W. Mission St
- Request within **10 days** of notice date
- Channels: in person, mail, email, telephone
- Informal hearing within **2 business days** of request
- Review under **CVC §22852**
- Sergeant/supervisor review if owner disagrees (CVC 22852(e))
- **DOT** hearings separate: **(408) 975-3718**, 200 E. Santa Clara St
