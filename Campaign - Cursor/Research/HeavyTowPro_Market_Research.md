# **6. Light & Medium Duty â€” Problems, Fears & Friction**
The problems, fears, and friction points are the invisible barriers between a searcher and a phone call. Every friction point that goes unaddressed in the ad or on the landing page is a conversion that walks to a competitor.

## **6.1 Problems They Face**
- They don't know if their vehicle can be towed by the company they're calling â€” medium-duty operators especially get turned away by light-duty-only companies
- They're on a freeway or in an unsafe location and need to know the truck is coming NOW
- They can't load a slow desktop website â€” 90% of searches happen on mobile while physically stranded
- They've been ripped off by a towing company before â€” surprise charges, held vehicle for ransom, bill far higher than quoted
- Insurance coverage is unclear â€” they don't know if this will be covered or out of pocket
- Night and weekend breakdowns create extra fear: 'Is anyone even answering this number?'

- RV and motorhome owners struggle to find a company that can actually handle their vehicle

## **6.2 Psychological Fears & Resistance Points**

| **Fear of Being Scammed / Price Gouged** The towing industry has a documented scam problem. Predatory operators charge inflated rates, hold vehicles, or add fees post-service. '15-minute average' with 50,000+ tows as social proof.** |
| --- | --- |

| **Fear of Vehicle Damage** Especially acute for luxury cars, motorcycles, low-clearance vehicles, and AWD vehicles. Real testimonial: 'My bike arrived without a scratch.'** | **Fear of Uncertainty / Loss of Control** Being stranded removes a person's sense of control.
